About Me

Hello! I’m a versatile full-stack developer passionate about building innovative and efficient digital solutions. With a solid foundation in front-end and back-end technologies, as well as SAP ABAP, I've honed my skills through hands-on experience and intensive training. Currently, I’m expanding my technical expertise through full-stack development projects. At Coding Ninjas Bootcamp, I’ve built applications ranging from a music player to an IMDb clone, mastering both front-end (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React) and back-end (Node.js, Express, MongoDB) technologies. My focus on object-oriented programming has enabled me to create efficient, scalable algorithms, ensuring robust code across projects. Previously, at Accenture, I worked on Seeburger application monitoring, managing complex incident resolutions, optimizing processes, and enhancing performance in both cloud and on-premise setups. My role involved close client interaction, incident management, and technical problem-solving—developing my adaptability and communication skills within a fast-paced environment. On this blog, I share my journey, projects, and insights on all things tech. I’m eager to connect with like-minded individuals, learn from the community, and contribute through knowledge sharing. Thanks for stopping by!
